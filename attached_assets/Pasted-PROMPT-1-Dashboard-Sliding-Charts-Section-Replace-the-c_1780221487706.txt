PROMPT 1 — Dashboard Sliding Charts Section
Replace the current "Recent Sales" bar chart on the dashboard with a horizontal swipeable/slideable chart carousel. Each slide contains one full chart. The admin swipes left or right to view the next chart. Show dot indicators at the bottom so the admin knows which slide they are on.
Include these slides in this order:
Slide 1 — Net Profit & Expenses Gauge
The semicircular gauge already built showing Net Profit in the center, Gross Profit on the left, Expenses on the right. Period selector: This Week / This Month / This Year.
Slide 2 — Revenue Over Time (Line Chart)
Smooth line chart showing daily revenue over the selected period. X-axis shows dates, Y-axis shows RWF amounts. Toggle buttons: This Week / This Month / This Year. Use real sales data from the database.
Slide 3 — Sales by Category (Donut Chart)
Donut/pie chart showing revenue percentage split between Phones, Computers, and Accessories. Show legend with category name, percentage, and total RWF amount below the chart.
Slide 4 — Top Selling Products (Horizontal Bar Chart)
Top 5 products ranked by revenue generated. Each bar shows product name on the left and RWF amount on the right. Use real sales data.
Slide 5 — Gross Profit vs Expenses (Area Chart)
Two overlapping shaded area lines — one teal for Gross Profit, one red/coral for Expenses — plotted over time. Shows visually when expenses were higher than profit.
Slide 6 — Payment Method Breakdown (Donut Chart)
Split of total sales by payment method: Cash, MoMo, Bank Transfer, Credit, Other. Show RWF totals per method in the legend.
Slide 7 — Outstanding Credit Summary
Not a chart — a styled card showing:
Total credit amount outstanding (RWF)
Number of customers with unpaid credit
Most recent credit sale (customer name + amount + date)
Oldest unpaid credit (customer name + how many days overdue)

Slide 8 — Monthly Target Progress
A horizontal progress bar showing sales progress toward a monthly target. Admin can set the target from Settings. Show percentage achieved and RWF remaining to target.
All charts must use real data from the database. Use Recharts or Chart.js (whichever is already in the project). The carousel must be swipeable on mobile (touch swipe) and also have left/right arrow buttons for desktop. Each slide has a title at the top.
