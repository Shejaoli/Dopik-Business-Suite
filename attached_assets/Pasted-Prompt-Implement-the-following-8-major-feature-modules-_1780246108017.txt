Their own isolated data — no tenant can see another tenant's data
Their own admin users, products, customers, sales, purchases, repairs
Their own logo, shop name, and branding colors (configurable in their settings)

Add a tenants table:
id, name, subdomain, logo_url, primary_color, address, phone, email, plan (basic/standard/premium), status (active/suspended/trial), created_at, trial_ends_at
